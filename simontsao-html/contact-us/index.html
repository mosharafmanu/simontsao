Contacting the Clinic

Booking an appointment with our clinic is simple and confidential.

  • Please complete the form below to book an appointment.

  • Our clinic operates Monday to Friday and we endeavour to respond within 24 hours of recieving this form. While we primarily work within business hours, there may be times when we may attempt to contact you outside of that time.

  • If an urgent appointment is needed, please call (03) 9133 6639 during business hours.

  • If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please dial 000 or attend your nearest emergency department.

Our Team

Our experienced team assists patients with enquiries, bookings, and ongoing coordination of care. They are committed to providing a supportive and professional point of contact for patients and families.

Emma Chittick

Emma is the first point of contact for our patients and at the heart of the clinic.

She brings broad experience across healthcare settings, including radiation oncology, diagnostic imaging, and allied health. Her organisational capability and commitment to patient care ensure that administrative processes run smoothly and efficiently. Emma cares deeply about our patients, which is reflected in her attention to detail, whether she's proactively following up on test results, coordinating appointments, or ensuring every patient interaction is handled with clarity, discretion, and respect.

Emma's background also includes work in equine care and racing, where she was involved in the management of leading stables and a government-qualified sample collection official on race days. Outside of work, she is a mum to three energetic boys and an active member of her local sporting community, including the treasurer of her local cricket club and team manager at the football club. Emma has completed a Diploma in Community Services, reflecting her ongoing commitment to compassionate, patient-centred care.
